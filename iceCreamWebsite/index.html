Locally Owned and Operated for Decades

Since we started in 1945, Brain Freeze Ice Cold Treats has been providing the Seattle area with tasty treats all year round. With six locations in the Seattle area, our array of fun flavors will totally knock your socks off! In addition to our handmade, organic ice cream, we also have a variety of other treats such as milkshakes, sundaes and more! If you want to satisfy your sweet tooth, we're the place for you!

Fan Favorite Flavors

We use all natural and organic ingredients to create delicious tasting treats for you and your family. From our seasonal staples to our monthly specials, there'll surely be something to please your taste buds.
