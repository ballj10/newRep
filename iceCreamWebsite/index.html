Since we started in 1945, Brain Freeze Ice Cold Treats has been providing the Seattle area with tasty summer delights. We have an array of fun flavors for you to try, from the classic to the inventive, as well as bars, cakes and milkshakes. If you want to satisfy your sweet tooth, come on down!
